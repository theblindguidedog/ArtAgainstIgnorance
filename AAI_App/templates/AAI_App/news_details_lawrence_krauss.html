Lawrence Krauss Tweets Song

Theoretical physicist and author Lawrence Krauss has tweeted our fun parody song 'what if you're wrong' which was co-written by Richard Dawkins. The song was forwarded to him by a mutual friend @FreethoughtCity. Lawrence then proceeded to give the song a shout out on Twitter which was an extremely pleasant surprise!
